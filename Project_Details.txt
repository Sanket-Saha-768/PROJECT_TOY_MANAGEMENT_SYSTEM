Toy shop management system
• Users are customers and admin. Only one admin allowed. Admin can also
    have a password.
• Information of toys can be read in from a file/from user and purchase receipts
    can be printed onto a file/screen. (Will be from a CSV)
• Admin can create, update or delete a toy and its details.
• Customers can see the information of the available toys that have been added
    by the admin.
• Customers can buy a toy only if its information is available.
• A small number of copies of each toy is available.
• When a toy is purchased print out all relevant information onto a file/screen,
    including a unique receipt number for each customer.(Using Current System Time and Date to the second as ID)
